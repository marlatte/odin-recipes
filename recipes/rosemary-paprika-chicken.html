Rosemary-Paprika Chicken

Description

This simple Spanish dish is minimalist and super easy to make. All you need are good vegetables and a few high-quality spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of chicken breasts
  • 3 garlic cloves, grated
  • Sea salt and black pepper
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp fresh rosemary (1/2 tsp dried)
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees, and place a sheet pan on the oven's lowest rack to heat.
  2. In liquid measuring cup, mix garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, rosemary, red pepper, olive oil, lemon juice, and zest.
  3. Chop chicken into bite-sized pieces
  4. Place chicken in bowl and pour spice mix on top. Be sure to mix evenly and completely. Let sit while the oven is heating up.
  5. Pour contents of bowl onto the heated sheet pan and spread it so that the chicken is spaced evenly.
  6. Place pan in the oven and let roast for 15 minutes, then flip chicken pieces over and roast for another 15 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and let rest for at least 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with a pinch of rosemary, paprika and lemon juice.
